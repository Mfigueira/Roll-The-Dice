GAME RULES

  • Set the WINNING SCORE (default is 50 points)
  • Each player in it's turn rolls a dice as many times as wished. Results adds up to the ROUND until the player 'Holds' that score to it's TOTAL. Then it's the next Player's turn
  • If a Player rolls a 1 or double 6, all it's ROUND score (not yet held) gets lost, and then it's the next Player's turn
  • The first Player to reach the FINAL SCORE wins!
